Support Privacy Policy

Support

Need a hand? We're happy to help.

Email us at support@thehonestappcompany.com. We typically reply within 2–3 business days.

How the free trial works

Honest Receipts is free to try for 3 days with the whole app unlocked. After the trial, a single one-time purchase unlocks it for good — no subscription and no auto-renew. Anything you already saved stays viewable and exportable even before you buy; we never lock you out of your own records.

I bought it on another device / reinstalled — how do I get it back?

Open the app, go to the unlock screen, and tap Restore Purchases. Your one-time purchase is tied to your Apple ID and restores for free.

Does the app read my receipts? Are they sent anywhere?

The app can fill in the merchant, total, and date from a photo using Apple's on-device text recognition. That scanning happens entirely on your phone — the image and the text never leave your device and are never sent to us.

How do reminders work?

When you add a warranty length or a return window, the app schedules a local notification on your device to remind you before that deadline. You can set the lead time, and turn reminders off, in Settings.

Where is my data? Can I export it?

Everything lives on your device. You can export a PDF of any item, a CSV of everything, or a full ZIP backup (every item's PDF, photos, and attached documents) at any time from the share menu — including after a trial ends.

Privacy Policy

Short version: we collect nothing. Your data stays on your phone.

Honest Receipts is designed so there's nothing to collect.

  • On-device only. Your receipts, warranties, photos, attached documents, tags, and reminders are stored locally on your device. They are never uploaded to us or anyone else.
  • On-device scanning. Reading the merchant, total, and date from a receipt photo uses Apple's on-device text recognition. The image and the recognized text never leave your phone.
  • No account. There is no sign-up and no login. We never ask for your email or identity.
  • No tracking, no analytics. We do not track your behavior, and we include no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • No servers. We don't operate a backend that receives your receipts or documents, because the app doesn't have one.
  • The only network use is the App Store. Purchasing or restoring the unlock goes through Apple's StoreKit. That transaction carries no receipt or document data — only what Apple needs to process a purchase.

App Store privacy label

This maps to "Data Not Collected." If that ever needs to change, we will say so plainly here and in the App Store listing first.

Sharing

When you tap Share or export, the PDF, CSV, or ZIP you created is handed to Apple's standard share sheet so you can send it where you choose (Mail, Messages, Files, AirDrop, Print). We are not involved in that transfer.

Warranty & return reminders

Honest Receipts records what you enter and reminds you before a deadline. It does not provide legal, insurance, or warranty advice, and it does not contact any retailer or manufacturer on your behalf.

Children

Honest Receipts is a personal utility intended for adults and is not directed to children. We collect no data from anyone.

Changes

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the App Store listing before the change ships.
